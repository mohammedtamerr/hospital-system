Prime Care Specialist Hospital Group is a pioneer in specialized medical care. Founded in 2000, we opened the first fully integrated private hospital in the Eastern Region, expanding into a globally ranked network committed to quality, safety, and patient experience.

We offer a comprehensive spectrum of care — from primary and specialized outpatient services to critical care, rehabilitation, home care, and a modern pharmacy network. Ranked among the top hospitals in the Gulf for patient satisfaction.
